About The Event

About The Event

Techign Summit is a two-day event being curated by Luova Foundation Team, with an expected audience of about 400. Our goal is to bring together bright minds to give talks that are design & idea-focused, and on a wide range of subjects, to foster learning, inspiration and wonder – and provoke conversations that matter. With the motive of educating young women to cherish in the field of designing Ms. Aiswarya Raju laid the stone for Luova community and paved the way for it. On 26th of July 2020 it was officially inaugurated. We work to setup a focused platform in educating and to provide them with astounding opportunities. Luova has successfully marked a year of establishment in the field of designing for women. With all of the strong support, co-operation and trust Luova Foundation introducing its 1st Annual Summit “Techign Summit 2021” on July 25th and 26th. The summit mainly comprises of Tech Talks, Panel Discussion, Non-Tech Talks and a few more activities.
